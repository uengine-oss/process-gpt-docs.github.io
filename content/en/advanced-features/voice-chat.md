# Voice Agent and Document/Slide Generation

## 1.1 Voice Chat-Based ProcessGPT

**Voice-based ProcessGPT** is a feature that provides requirement-based answers at the speed of real conversation through reduced interface latency using WebSocket, based on information created in the tenant.<br>

Voice-based ProcessGPT identifies the process list and to-do list registered in the tenant to find and answer the information users need, making it easy to understand the complex process information that accumulates over time. <br>
Additionally, when users request processes and requirements via voice, it can generate detailed task definitions and their contents.


### 1.2 Viewing Process Lists

The method of checking process lists using voice chat is as follows.

Click the 'Chat' icon at the top to enter the chat screen, then check if an Assistant has been created. If it hasn't been created, add System as a participant through chat room creation and check if the screen below appears.<br>
![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/1.png)
<br>
<br>
<br>

Click the 'Speak/Listen' icon at the bottom of the message input area to enter the voice chat screen.<br>
![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/2.png)<br>

Click the microphone button and proceed with a voice conversation asking about the process you want to check, such as 'Explain the contract management process among the created processes.'<br>
![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/3.png)<br>

After the conversation is complete, the AI will provide voice answers about each task and connected form information for the contract management process among registered processes, and you can check the AI's answer in the chat.<br>
![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/4.png)<br>

### 1.2 Process Planning

The method of planning a vacation request process using voice chat is as follows.

Click the 'Speak/Listen' icon at the bottom of the message input area to enter the voice chat screen.
![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/2.png)<br>
Click the microphone button and make a request about the process you want to plan, such as 'Plan the detailed tasks for planning a vacation request process.'<br>

![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/3.png)<br>
The more specific requirements you add to your request, the more detailed the task planning will be, so provide detailed requirements for the process you want to plan.

After the conversation is complete, the AI will provide step-by-step voice answers based on the detailed tasks and form contents included in the vacation request process, and you can check the AI's answer in the chat.<br>

Through this, you can quickly understand internally generated information and receive detailed guidance on specific steps from AI during process planning, improving work efficiency and process quality.


### 1.3 Use Case: Reading Conversation Context and Immediate ROI Analysis (Context Awareness)
The moment questions like "What's the ROI of this project?" or "Which is more advantageous, Plan A or Plan B?" come up during a meeting, AI is not just a bystander. The following shows the process where AI participates in the chat room, reads the context, and performs calculations on its own.

#### 1.3.1 Agent Invitation and Listening
The user invites a specialized AI agent such as **'ROI Calculation Assistant'** to the business chat room with colleagues. The agent does not directly intervene in the conversation but loads the incoming information (costs, conditions, etc.) into memory in real-time.

![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/5.png)
- Inviting **'ROI Calculation Assistant'** to the business chat room and starting conversation context sharing
<br>
<br>
<br>

#### 1.3.2 Context Understanding and Autonomous Intervention (Triggering)
During the conversation, quote conditions from Company A and Company B are exchanged as text. The user does not need to separately tell the AI "Calculate" and re-enter the data. The moment a natural language question like "Which one is better if we use it for 3 years?" comes up, the AI has already understood the context of the previous conversation (Company A/B conditions) and immediately starts calculating.

![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/6.png)
- Even when the user only asks a question without entering data, AI prepares to intervene immediately based on the previous conversation content
<br>
<br>
<br>

#### 1.3.3 Immediate Calculation Result Presentation (Answer)
AI performs calculations immediately in the background and displays the calculated figures (total cost comparison) and the basis for judgment on screen. It supports decision-making by including not only simple calculations but also qualitative context such as "Company B's features are superior."

![](../../../uengine-image/process-gpt/voice-chat/7.png)
- Comparative analysis results derived immediately from the conversation flow without separate search or calculation execution
<br>
<br>

## 2. Voice Agent & Call Bot: Achieving a True Hands-Free Environment

Moving away from the traditional method where users directly view the screen and enter data, complex input processes are resolved through speech, and the agent proactively makes calls to verify necessary information and automatically reflects it in the system.
